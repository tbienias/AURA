GOAL:
Transfer image (and other) data between HoloLens and remote companion programm.

INITIAL IDEA:
Use some MQ Library for easy and reliable communication that could be extended to more complex protocols. 

PROBLEM:
Most popular high level messaging librarys (i.e. ZeroMQ) are not awailable for UWP.

FALLBACK ON BUILDINS:
Basic overview of the networking technologies available for an UWP app
https://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/uwp/networking/which-networking-technology

DECISION:
use Windows.Networking.Sockets

EXAMPLES:
https://github.com/Microsoft/Windows-universal-samples/tree/master/Samples/StreamSocket
